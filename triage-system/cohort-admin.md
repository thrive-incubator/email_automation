## Program identity

- **Official name:** The Practical Certificate in IECMH Consultation (Infant & Early Childhood Mental Health Consultation). Also referred to in emails as the "IECMHC Training," "IECMH Consultation Certificate," "foundational/founding cohort," and the "Practical Certificate program." Use **"Practical Certificate in IECMH Consultation"** in formal replies.
- **Run by:** Georgetown University **Thrive Center for Children, Families, and Schools** (a.k.a. Georgetown Thrive Center). The operating/billing entity is **Attunify, Inc. ("Attunify by Thrive Center")**.
- **Format:** Fully virtual. A mix of **self-paced learning modules** (in a learning management system / "learning center") plus **weekly live 90-minute classes** over Zoom.
- **Brand-new program:** This is the founding run. Several systems (LMS, login flow) are still being set up, so it's honest and on-brand to say "this is a brand-new program and a few details are still settling."

## Access / login / LMS

- The **learning management system (LMS) is brand-new and being set up.** Login and account-setup instructions are **sent from the LMS itself**, not manually. The honest message to candidates: "Everything — including how to set up your account and log in — will be sent to you from the learning system. We're finalizing that setup now."
- The **live classes use a recurring Zoom link** that candidates receive (from the learning center / next-steps email).
- ⚠️ Do not invent or paste an LMS URL, login credentials, or a Zoom link. We don't have a confirmed candidate-facing URL on record — if asked for the link directly, say it will come from the learning system / we'll make sure they get it.

## Cadence / format details

- Live classes are **interactive, not lectures** — designed for asking questions, practicing scenarios, and applying the material. Candidates are expected to **complete that week's self-paced modules before the live session.** Good phrasing: "Each week you'll work through the self-paced modules, then join the 90-minute live class to ask questions and practice."
- There is **supervision / post-program support** as part of the program (a candidate asked for "clarification on supervision and post-program support"). ⚠️ Details of the supervision model and post-program support are **not fully specified in the inbox** — if asked specifics, give the high level ("reflective supervision and post-program support are part of the program") and offer to have someone follow up with detail. Shai: please add the exact supervision structure here.

## Team changes (adding / swapping members)

- Teams can **add members** (e.g. Oregon AIMH added a 5th team member). When someone asks to add a person, the path is: get the new person's name and email, send them the class details/times, and ⚠️ confirm whether an added seat changes billing (likely yes — route the cost question to billing). Don't confirm "no extra charge"; hand pricing for an added seat to billing.
- ⚠️ Confirm with Shai whether there's a team-size cap (some teams have 4–5). Don't quote a cap we're unsure of.
