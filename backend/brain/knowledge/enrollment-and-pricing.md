## What the program is (the short pitch)

- **The Practical Certificate in IECMH Consultation** — a **fully virtual** certificate program from the **Georgetown Thrive Center**, operated by **Attunify, Inc. ("Attunify by Thrive Center")**.
- Format: **self-paced learning modules + weekly live 90-minute classes** over Zoom, with reflective supervision and post-program support. (See cohort-admin.md for schedule specifics.)
- It's the founding run of a brand-new program, intentionally designed to be **accessible to people from a range of professional backgrounds**, not just clinicians.

## Eligibility (the most common question — lead with inclusion)

- **A graduate degree is NOT strictly required.** Verbatim grounding: *"Not necessarily. The whole point is to open it up to people from more backgrounds. So if you have someone (for example) with a degree in education, they could be eligible as long as they have years of [relevant] experience."*
- Practical framing for replies: relevant education **plus** meaningful experience working with young children/families can qualify someone even without a clinical graduate degree.
- ⚠️ We don't have a precise, written eligibility rubric (exact years of experience, which degrees, any licensure). **Don't state hard cutoffs.** When someone describes their background, affirm warmly and offer to confirm fit rather than quoting a strict rule.
